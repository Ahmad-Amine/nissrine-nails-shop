NAIL PHOTOS — HOW TO ADD YOUR OWN PHOTOS
==========================================

Place your nail photos in this folder with these exact filenames:

  nail-hero.jpg        → Main large photo in the hero section (recommended: 600x400px)
  nail-small-1.jpg     → Small grid photo 1 (recommended: 300x300px)
  nail-small-2.jpg     → Small grid photo 2
  nail-small-3.jpg     → Small grid photo 3
  nail-small-4.jpg     → Small grid photo 4
  gallery-1.jpg        → Gallery section photo 1 (recommended: 500x500px)
  gallery-2.jpg        → Gallery section photo 2
  gallery-3.jpg        → Gallery section photo 3
  gallery-4.jpg        → Gallery section photo 4
  gallery-5.jpg        → Gallery section photo 5
  gallery-6.jpg        → Gallery section photo 6

WHERE TO GET FREE PROFESSIONAL NAIL PHOTOS:
  - https://unsplash.com/s/photos/nail-manicure  (free, no account needed)
  - https://www.pexels.com/search/nail%20art/     (free, no account needed)

HOW TO ADD:
  1. Download any nail photo
  2. Rename it to match the filename above (e.g. nail-hero.jpg)
  3. Drop it into this folder
  4. Refresh your browser — the photo appears instantly!

SUPPORTED FORMATS: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp
